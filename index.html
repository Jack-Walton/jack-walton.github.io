Music Information Retrieval in Bandcamp
A graduate paper for the University of Missouri. The paper is an APA-style, UX literature review. At the time, I was enrolled as a library and information science student. The paper received full marks from three peer reviewers and the professor. In this paper, I analyze the Bandcamp information system against peer-reviewed literature to derive auspicious web design ideas.
Summer Undergraduate Research Opportunity Grant Proposal
A grant proposal I wrote while an undergraduate researcher. The travel grant, which was worth $1250, was not funded due to the 2020 lockdown. However, the university did award me a certificate of achievement for the quality of the document.
HIM Configuration Help
A brief instruction manual that I wrote for Oracle Health. The manual is for a cloud-based application that integrates the Optum web encoder with the Cerner Health Information Management (HIM) solution. This technology helps streamline the highly-regulated financial transactions between clinics and insurance companies. It also forms the backbone of an accurate electronic health record. Subject-matter expertise was provided by the Cerner HIM product and engineering teams. Editorial guidance was prodived by my documentation architect. It is hosted on the Cerner Confluence instance, CernerWiki.
